Thai Green Curry

A vibrant, aromatic curry with tender chicken (or your choice of protein), vegetables, and a rich coconut milk base. It's perfect for a comforting meal with a hint of spice.

Ingredients
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 400g chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 200ml chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bamboo shoots
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1-2 red chilies, sliced
  • 1 lime (for zest and juice)
  • Cooked jasmine rice, for serving

Method
  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© for 3-4 minutes until softened.

  2. Add the sliced chicken (or tofu) to the pan and cook for about 5-6 minutes, until browned and cooked through.

  3. Stir in the green curry paste and cook for 1-2 minutes, letting it release its fragrance.

  4. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth, and bring to a simmer. Stir in the fish sauce, brown sugar, and sliced red bell pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  5. Add the zucchini and bamboo shoots (if using), and simmer for another 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.

  6. Stir in fresh basil leaves, lime juice, and zest for a burst of freshness. Add sliced red chilies for extra heat, if desired.

  7. Serve the curry hot, over a bed of fluffy jasmine rice. Garnish with extra basil or lime wedges if preferred.

  8. Dive into this flavorful, aromatic curry!
