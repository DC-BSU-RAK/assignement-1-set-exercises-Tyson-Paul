Characters

  • Jake Sully: A former Marine who becomes an avatar operator on Pandora. Jake's journey from a mission-driven soldier to a committed defender of the Na'vi people forms the emotional core of the narrative.​
  • Neytiri: A skilled Na'vi warrior and princess of the Omaticaya clan. She becomes Jake's mentor and eventual partner, guiding him into the ways of her people and Pandora's natural world.
  • Dr. Grace Augustine: A scientist and head of the Avatar Program. She is dedicated to studying Pandora's ecosystem and advocating for peaceful relations with the Na'vi.
  • Col. Quaritch: The head of security for the human operations on Pandora. He is a hardened military leader who views the Na'vi as obstacles to human expansion and resources.​
  • Parker Selfridge: The corporate administrator overseeing the human mining operation on Pandora. His primary concern is profit, often at the expense of ethical considerations.
  • Trudy Chacón: A combat pilot who supports the Avatar Program. She is sympathetic to the Na'vi and becomes an ally to Jake and Grace.
  • Eytukan: The leader of the Omaticaya clan and Neytiri's father. He is initially wary of humans but is open to understanding their intentions.Spiritual and clan leaders
